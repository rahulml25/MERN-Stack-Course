Ideal Institute of Engineering, Kalyani

The Ideal Institute of Engineering (IIE) stands as a testament to the visionary pursuit of The Ideal Science and Research Welfare Hub, conceived to establish a center of excellence in technical education. Founded in 2010, the institute received AICTE approval and affiliation from Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), officially embarking on its academic journey in 2011.

At IIE, we cultivate intellect, inspire creativity, and empower minds, shaping professionals who will redefine the frontiers of technology and drive the progress of our nation.
